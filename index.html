About Me

Ethan Charbonniere — Game Developer & Game Designer

My name is Ethan Charbonniere, I’m a game developer and designer with a Bachelor’s degree in Game Design and Creative Coding from E-Artsup Lyon (2025). Passionate about crafting unique gameplay experiences, I have a particular love for roguelike and roguelite games.

On the technical side, I handle gameplay code, systems, asset integration, and editor tools. On the design side, I structure mechanics, difficulty, level design, and in-game UX.
